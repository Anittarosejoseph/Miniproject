Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I submit a watch repair request?

You can fill out our online form in the 'contact us' section on this website. We'll provide you with a tentative estimate based on the information you provide.

2. What warranty do you provide for watch repairs?

We provide a 1-year warranty for all watch repairs, ensuring that any issues post-repair will be promptly resolved at no additional cost (terms and conditions apply).

3. Can I bring my watch in person or should I courier it?

You can either bring your watch in person to one of our stores in Delhi or courier the watch to us.

4. Are you an authorized service center for specific brands?

We are an authorized service center of Seiko, Citizen, and Victorinox. While we are not authorized for other brands, we do offer repair services for them.

5. Do you repair clocks?

Yes, we repair clocks! Our clock repair services include battery replacement, movement repair, and cleaning. Contact us to discuss your clock repair needs.

6. When should I service my watch?

If your watch is losing time, running fast, making unusual noises, or it has been more than three years since your last service, we recommend having it serviced to ensure longevity.

7. Can you replace broken watch crystals?

Yes, we can replace broken watch crystals. We offer a variety of crystal types and can help you select the best option for your watch.

8. Do you repair vintage and antique watches?

Yes, we have experience repairing vintage and antique watches. Our watchmakers are skilled in working with a variety of watch styles and can help restore your vintage or antique watch to its former glory.

9. How can I repair a watch online?

To repair a watch online, you can follow these steps:

  • Fill out our online watch repair form with details about the issues you're facing.
  • Send your watch to us through a secure courier service.
  • Our expert watchmakers will assess the issues and provide you with a cost estimate.
  • Upon approval, we will proceed with the repair and keep you updated on the progress.
  • Once the repair is complete, we'll securely ship your watch back to you.
Note: Online watch repair services may vary, and it's important to choose a reputable service provider.
